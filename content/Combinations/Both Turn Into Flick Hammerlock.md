## Steps

1. [[Right Turn]] for the follower, while holding both hands up.
1. On 6, while the follower is turning, the leader steps sideways instead of to the back.
1. On 7, the follower should have finished most of the turn and give space for the leader to turn left underneath the arms.
1. [[Flick Hammerlock]]
