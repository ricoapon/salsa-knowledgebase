This is not just a [[Right Turn]] but the other direction. The leader needs to guide the follower into this movement with shoulder movement. Note that the leader can also do a Left Turn, which doesn't need this shoulder movement, because the follower just continues with a [[Basic Step]].

It is often used in this pattern: [[Left Turn]], then the leader does a [[Right Turn]] into a [[Right Turn]] for the follower. This is also shown in the video.
