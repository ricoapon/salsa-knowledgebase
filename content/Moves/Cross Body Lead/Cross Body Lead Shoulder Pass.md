This is a variant of the [[Cross Body Lead]] where the leader starts with [[Left to Left Hand Hold]]. Instead of making a step forward on 1, the leader steps to the right side of the follower. It is sort of a reverse turn. Lead your left hand over your shoulder. You end up with a normal [[Cross Body Lead]] and can continue on 5 with the normal steps.
