This is a variant where the leader prepares the sideways movement earlier and does a [[Cumbia]] with the additional time.

It works as follows (follower does [[Basic Step]] on all beats):

| Beat | Leader                                                                                     |
| ---- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 5    | Regular [[Basic Step]]                                                                     |
| 6    | Leader places the left foot to the right of the follower.                                  |
| 7    | Close the feet. Now you are in a regular [[Cross Body Lead]] position, but 4 counts early. |
| 1    | Leader does a [[Cumbia]].                                                                  |
| 2    |                                                                                            |
| 3    | Now continue with regular [[Cross Body Lead]]                                              |
