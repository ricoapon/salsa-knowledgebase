This move is one of the most fundamental moves used in salsa. It is a move in which the leader and follower switch positions. It is also common to use this move to switch the hand position.

The simplest form works as follows:

1. Regular [[Basic Step]] for both.
2. Leader takes the backward step sideways to the left. Follower continues with the [[Basic Step]].
3. Leader puts feat next to each other and follower finishes the step. Now there is a 90 degree angle between leader and follower.
5. Leader guides, with the right hand, the follower forward. Leader steps in place.
6. Leader steps with the left foot next to the backwards foot of the follower. Follower turns towards the leader with this step.
7. Both finish with feet together.
