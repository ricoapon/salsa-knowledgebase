This is a variant of the [[Cross Body Lead]] where you start with a [[Crosshold]]. The leader makes the regular [[Cross Body Lead]] steps, but some additional hand movement on 1 and 2. On 1, the leader does a [[Hair Comb]] using the right hand over the head of the follower. Then on 2, the leader does a [[Hair Comb]] using the left hand over the head of the leader. Then finish the regular [[Cross Body Lead]].
