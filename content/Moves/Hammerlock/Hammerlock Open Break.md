---
title: Hammerlock Open Break
tags:
  - learned
---

Do a [[Right Turn]] from a [[Basic Step]], but keep holding both hands. This gets the follower into a [[Hammerlock]]. Now guide the follower to your right, both stepping forwards and turning towards each other. Because of this movement, it is logical to make an [[Open Break]] step.
