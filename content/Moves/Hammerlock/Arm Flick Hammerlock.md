When doing a [[Cross Body Lead]], on 1, the leader raises the right hand and then "flicks" the hand downwards behind her back. This should have the follower go into a [[Hammerlock]] position. Now continue with [[Hammerlock Cross Body Lead Inside Turn]].

Note: I couldn't find a good video for only showing this. I also don't think it is called "flick". The workshop video shows this as part of the combination.
